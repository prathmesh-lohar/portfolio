PanditPlus – Online Pandit Booking

This page contains information about the PanditPlus platform including project overview, features, tools used, and more.

Live Link

Project Overview

PanditPlus is an online pandit booking platform that connects Yajmans (users) with qualified and experienced Pandits for various religious ceremonies, rituals, and spiritual services.

The system includes two dedicated interfaces—one for Pandits and one for Yajmans—ensuring a smooth, easy, and well-organized booking and service management experience.

Key Features

  1. 1) Dual Interfaces: Separate dashboards for Pandits and Yajmans.
  2. 2) Easy Pandit Booking: Quickly book pandits for pujas and rituals.
  3. 3) Pooja Categorization: Griha Pravesh, Satyanarayan, Rudra Abhishek, Laxmi Pooja, Vastu Shanti & more.
  4. 4) Service Personalization: Custom suggestions based on rituals, dates & location.
  5. 5) Booking Management: Track upcoming & past bookings easily.
  6. 6) Responsive UI: Fully mobile-friendly modern interface.
